The Negative Health Aspects of Social Media on Two Generations; A Comparative Analysis Between Generation Y (Millennials) and Generation Z (Gen Z)

In today's current generation, social media has revolutionized how people communicate and consume information. Social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter have become an integral part of today's daily lives for millions of individuals, specifically among younger generations, namely Generation Z. According to (n.a, 2023) 54% of Gen Zs use social media 4 hours a day, and the other 38% use those social platforms more than 4 hours. We all know that it is evident that Generation Z is more socially inclined and dependent on social media, which makes this generation curious about its negative health effects.
